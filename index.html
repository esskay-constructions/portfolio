About Us

ESSKAY Constructions, established by Shri. Kishore Kumar D (Managing Partner) in 2014, stands as a cutting-edge infrastructure development, construction, and project management company. We proudly present ourselves as a leading construction firm, having successfully executed a prestigious projects in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. Our commitment to delivering projects on time, coupled with a focus on superior quality and finishing, defines our track record. Additionally, our team of qualified engineers brings extensive experience, ensuring compliance with both Indian and international standards. ESSKAY Constructions collaborates closely with clients, contributing to the creation of high-quality, sustainable property assets that have a positive impact on society and the global community.
